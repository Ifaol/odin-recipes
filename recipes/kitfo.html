Kitfo

How to prepare kitfo, a traditional Ethiopian food.

Kitfo, a unique Ethiopian dish, is crafted by finely mincing fresh, lean beef. In a bowl, season the meat with nigella seeds, cardamom, and spicy mitmita blend, adjusting the spice to your preference. A drizzle of clarified butter binds the flavors together.

Traditionally served with injera, Kitfo offers a distinct culinary adventure, highlighting the bold and vibrant taste of Ethiopian cuisine. Enjoy the harmonious blend of spices and the exquisite, raw beef, making Kitfo a memorable dish that stands out in the world of culinary exploration.

Ingredients

  • Quality lean beef, finely minced
  • Nigella seeds (also known as "ketema" in Amharic)
  • Ground cardamom
  • Mitmita spice blend (a mixture of chili peppers and spices)
  • Clarified butter (niter kibbeh)
  • Salt
  • Injera (traditional sourdough flatbread)

Steps

  1. Season the Beef
  2. Add Clarified Butter
  3. Serve with kocho
